About Our App

We developed an innovative AI Chatbot designed to manage appointment scheduling. Our virtual assistant will allow 24/7 availability, increased ease of booking and an increase in the number of appointments without having to hire additional staff.

Fully Resposive

BotLemon is designed for seamless conversations with customers. It's framework allows it to answer customer questions and book or cancel appointments. The appointments will be linked directly to your business' calendar to show the upcoming appointments BotLemon has scheduled.

24/7 Service

Our virtual assistant provides quick booking service available 24/7. Customers have instant access to book appointments or consultations from the ease of their home.

Voice and Text Based Response

BotLemon is designed for both Voice and Text based response. Having both avenues will help increase customer satisfaction and comfort while using our platform.

Easy To Customize

List all of your business' available services and offerings. Show prices, descriptions, pictures and/or videos to effectively market your different range of products.

Fully Integratable

Designed to work on any messenger platform. It can be integrated into Text, Phone Calls, Websites, Emails, FB Messenger, Slack, Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, Siri, and more. All customers will be able to use the service free of charge across a range of social media platforms.

Stylish Design

Our innovative marketing style attracts users to interact with the bot. This will help increase social media presence to reach the maximum amount of potential customers.
